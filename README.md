# Hatty's Universe 🌌

Welcome to **Hatty's Universe**, a fun, interactive 3D digital universe created to showcase all the exciting projects, platforms, and tools I use and am passionate about. 

## What is this?
Instead of a boring, traditional list of links, I wanted to build a world that you can actually explore. Every "planet" in this universe represents a different project, tool, or community. You can fly around the cosmos in 3D, take a guided tour, and discover new and exciting things just by interacting with the celestial bodies around you.

## Why did I create it?
I made this to be an engaging and visually stunning way to share my journey in the digital space. Whether it's learning resources, crypto platforms like Cardano and Voi, privacy gadgets, or content creation tools, this universe holds all of my current interests.

**The universe is constantly expanding!** As time goes on, new planets will be added to the cosmos, and we will see just how big we can make Hatty's Universe. 

## How to Explore
- **Desktop**: Click inside the universe, use `W/A/S/D` to fly around, hold `Shift` to boost, and drag your mouse to look.
- **Mobile**: Use 1 finger to look around and 2 fingers to fly, or use the on-screen controls. 
- You can hover over any planet to see its name, and click on it to warp straight to that project's page.
- Feeling adventurous? Click **Take a Tour** to sit back and watch an automated flight path through the entire solar system.

## Local Setup
If you want to run this locally:
1. Open the folder in your terminal.
2. Run a simple local server (e.g., `python -m http.server 8000` or `npx serve`).
3. Visit `http://localhost:8000` in your browser.
